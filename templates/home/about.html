Building the Future of Academic Monitoring

AT-PAS was developed as an AICT first semester main project with the primary goal of leveraging data science to solve real-world problems in student attendance management.

We aim to create a proactive feedback loop between students and faculty, minimizing academic risk through timely alerts and robust analytics.

OUR MISSION

The Problem-Solution Approach

Understanding the challenges and how AT-PAS addresses them

The Challenge
⚠️

Reactive Warnings

Students often receive warnings only after they have failed to meet the required compliance percentage, leaving little time for corrective action.

📋

Manual Reporting

Attendance data is isolated and difficult to visualize for trend analysis, making it hard to identify patterns early.

The Solution
🎯

Predictive Alerting

AI-powered predictions identify at-risk students before attendance drops below threshold, enabling proactive intervention.

📊

Visual Analytics

Real-time graphs and comprehensive dashboards powered by advanced data visualization make trends instantly clear.
